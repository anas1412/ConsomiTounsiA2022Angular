The delivery time is counted from the day you place your order until the day of our first delivery attempt.
Business Days are Monday through Saturday. They do not include Sundays and public holidays.
You can track your order by clicking on "My Account" at the top right of your screen.

ConsommiTounsi Express is a Premium service that concerns all products prepared and shipped by us. By purchasing ConsummiTounsi Express products, you benefit from a faster delivery time.

Standard delivery

ConsommiTounsi STANDARD products are products prepared by sellers on the ConsommiTounsi platform. These products are stored with the sellers and not with ConsummiTounsi. Before shipping your order, the seller delivers the product to us in stock and ConsummiTounsi takes care of checking the contents and shipping. This is why the delivery time is longer than that of ConsummiTounsi Express products.
