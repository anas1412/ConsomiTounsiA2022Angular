WHAT SHIPPING COSTS APPLY TO MY ORDER ?



In order to ensure you a better experience, delivery costs will now be calculated more optimally and will depend on the following 2 factors :
  • Delivery destination: delivery costs vary according to the distance between the city of dispatch and the city of delivery.
  • Weight / Size: delivery costs vary according to the weight and volume of the package. The lower the weight/volume the lower the fees you pay.

To know the delivery costs, simply select the product(s) and choose your delivery address. The charges will be visible at the bottom of the page before confirming your order.
